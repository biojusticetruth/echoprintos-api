About EchoprintOS

A lightweight, public-verifiable timestamping layer for posts and artifacts. Each entry receives an ECP (Echoprint Code + UTC time) and a hidden SHA-256 hash for integrity.

What you can do

  • Verify by ECP (e.g., ECP-YYYYMMDDHHMMSSmmm) or by 64-hex hash.
  • Browse the recent ledger and open a record permalink.
  • Optionally link to the original post (X, LinkedIn, Substack, etc.).

Privacy & Integrity

  • Public view exposes only: ECP, timestamp, title (optional), permalink (optional), and the content hash.
  • Writes are controlled; reads happen through a restricted SQL view.

Roadmap

  • Capture form → Make.com automations.
  • Record detail pages with share cards.
  • Independent audit & export tools.
